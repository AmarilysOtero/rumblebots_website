Meet The Robots

Sumo Bot

Mario

Requirements

The design is developed prioritizing manufacturability, part replaceability, and component protection. In addition, the modular aspect of the design is also to be considered throughout the whole design process.

This allows the team to develop several different weapons that can be replaced on one same robot body, permitting us to pick which weapon to use depending on our opponent.

  • 12-pound weight constraint
  • Being a modular robot
  • Manufacturability
  • Replaceability
  • Component protection

Development

The team began this semester by drawing inspiration from this previously developed concept but taking into account the established needs and requirements. This led to the creation of proof of concept prototypes, and the development of a much more manufacturable design of what was initially envisioned by the team.

Furthermore, the organization of departments in the team that worked directly with the development of the combat robot was modified to allow members to have a more holistic approach to solving engineering problems.
