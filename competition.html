The Competitions

Robot Ruckus

This competition takes place in Orlando, Florida, during the month of November. Robot Ruckus is part of Maker Faire and is the largest open heavyweight combat robot competition in the United States.

The team would have an opportunity to participate in 12 pound weight categories, as well as having weekend access to the overall Maker Faire event, giving the team’s members a chance to network with other engineers and makers alike.

Ecuador’s Robot Games Zero Latitude

Due to covid restrictions in the country, this competition is on hold. The team is currently in search of an adequate competition.

The team believes that a sumo bot competition would be an ideal first step to introduce compact robotics in the island for high school and college students.
