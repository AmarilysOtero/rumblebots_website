Join the UPRM Combact Robot team

We design, manufacture, test and optimize combat robots of varying weights to represent the UPRM and Puerto Rico at the Nationals Robotics League and other robotics competitions.

LegaZ

12-Pound

A 12-pound modular combat robot to represent the team and university in Ecuador’s Robot Games Zero Latitude international robotics competition.

Our members have the opportunity to design, analize, manufacture, finance and operate the robots.

Mario

Zumo Bot

Robot-sumo is a sport in which two robots attempt to push each other out of a circle called Dohyo, and much like the traditional Japanese martial art of Sumo wrestling.

Our team is currently building our own autonomus robot that consists of making a 500 grams robot that can face off against another one of its kind on a specified field of combat.
