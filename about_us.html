About RUMblebots

Our Mision

To complete development of combat robots while enabling its members to learn and apply engineering knowledge and concepts in a real, hands-on manner.

Our Vision

To create an environment in which every student can grow academically, mentally, and to help with the professional development of our members. One of RUMblebots ambitions is to introduce combat robotics in Puerto Rico and create a competition hosted in the University of Puerto Rico, Mayaguez Campus.

History

RUMblebots was established in 1994. The purpose and goal of the RUMblebots special project is the complete development of combat robots while enabling its members to learn and apply engineering knowledge and concepts in a real, hands-on manner.
